About ArtForCovid

A non-profit organization led by Indonesian youth aiming to help endangered animals in Indonesia who are even more at risk due to the pandemic. 100% profit donated to WWF and BOS Foundation!

Our journey.

April 2020.
ArtForCovid started as a two person project and we raised over IDR 11M for COVID-19 relief in 3 months by providing a custom digital illustrations in exchange of donations to Kitabisa’s fundraiser platform. The donations were used to provide necessities such as hazmat suits, food and water to healthcare professionals battling COVID-19.


June 2020.
We started another community service project with with the aim to “adopt” 1 orangutan per month from BOS Foundation by donating some of the profit we got from selling our art services.


August 2020.
We wanted to create a bigger impact and try something new, so we decided to bootstrap a social entrepreneurship to spread awareness on how orangutans are endangered by climate change, and forgotten in pandemic. We recruited 27 other selfless and passionate individuals into our team, so now we have a team of 29. We are now all working on selling merchandise such as t-shirts and stickers with COVID-19 and endangered animal designs made by us which we are selling on Shopee.
